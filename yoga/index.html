"Yoga infected me the moment I stepped on to a mat for the first time.

It had been years since I had danced professionally and I was growing tired of the gym. Working out was engaging enough, but there was something missing. There was no creativity. No inspiration. At the time, I had no idea yoga would end up providing those things for me... I just needed a new outlet for my energy. In fact, I think I stayed away from yoga for so long because of some perconceived notions that the practice would somehow not be physically challenging enough for me. Thinking back, I take pleasure in the reminder that things are not always as they seem, and that remaining open to try new things is actually the key everything."

People come to yoga for a plethora of reasons and I daresay that for most, yoga fulfils all those initial needs, but more often than not, it ends up transforming into something much deeper. The point however, is so simple:

Yoga will meet you wherever you are.
