I teach Hatha yoga, which for individual sessions will be tailored to your particular needs.

Hatha can be a vigorous workout (vinyasa style) or it can be a calm and relaxing experience (restorative) or an intricate combination of the two. What we work on will be a collaborative effort, meaning, I will ask you very detailed questions about your health and goals and I will custom tailor our sessions based on your needs... Knowing those needs may change by the month, week, day or even hour. I am always here to discuss where you are and what you are feeling, and change things up accordingly.


The only thing you need to remember is:

This is your practice. Beginners absolutely welcome!


On Teaching

Barbara has been practicing yoga for about 7 years now and although she still considers herself a student first and foremost, she has a great desire to share the depth of this practice with others.

"My inspiration comes from the idea that maaybe, just maybe, I can open the gateway for others the way my teachers did for me. If I can give others a taste of the unlimited riches I myself have found here, if I can help people tuen in to their own source of joy and give them a glimpse of their own strength & beauty, even just the tiniest bit, well that's compelling beyond words."
