Freestyle 1

How much can you see with your eyes closed?
meandering blindly through your existence
strangulated by despair’s dusky mist
misconception’s got you missin’
precious gifts that life’s givin’
Imagining happiness is great
living it is greater
crack open a bit of heaven’s smile
catch the feelin’, know why
why other people spelunk this cave of life
with seemingly little emotional ties
let go of yourself
consciously undo the subconscious
don’t allow yourself
to be subjugated
victim to yourself, devastated
the journey of yourself can be much clearer
the treasures of life are on the horizon.
