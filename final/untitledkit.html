Untitled
(Magnetic Poetry Kit Freestyle)

Imagine raw passion
Glorious and deep
With impressions
Of him
His chiseled silhouette
In her head
The picture composed
On wildly rigid canvas
Every painful icon shimmering
Like shimmering glass
A surreal masterpiece
Sculpted just for her
She remembers his gentle demeanor
His soft touch
His sweet breath on her neck
He was just too good to be true
They blended perfectly
Her desire for him was great
As strong as her desire to live
The memories grew more vivid
As she sketched him
She cumulated eight years of him
In pencil stroke after pencil stroke
