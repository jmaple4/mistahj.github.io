Untitled 3

Power inebriates those in charge
drunk with authority, leaving scars
on the hearts of those without it
stomping on people trying to re-route it
the highest power is absolute
it can’t be shared by a group
suffering is more of a group ethic
people like to compare how their lives are pathetic
complaining replaces the drive to get it
the potential is latent, no one wants to use it
anyone can have it
the power’s up for whoever grabs it
when mental capacity matches tenacity
beautiful things happen, if pursued passionately
